<Section>
  <Section.Title>Overview</Section.Title>
  <Section.Text>Badge is a small inline element that communicates status, category, or numeric quantity. It supports semantic color variants, three sizes, and three shape options, covering the most common labeling patterns.</Section.Text>
</Section>

<Section>
  <Section.Title>Variant</Section.Title>

</Section>

<Section>
  <Section.Title>Size</Section.Title>
  <Section.Text>Three sizes are available: `large`, `medium`, and `small`. The default is `medium`.</Section.Text>
  <ComponentExample name="badge-sizes" />
</Section>

<Section>
  <Section.Title>Shape</Section.Title>
  <Section.Text>Three shape options adjust the border-radius. Use `circle` for numeric quantity badges.</Section.Text>

</Section>
